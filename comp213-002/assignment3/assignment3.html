LG Electronics Smartphones

G Series - G7 ThinQ

View Larger Picture

The LG G7—officially named the LG G7 ThinQ—is the latest addition to LG Electronics' G-series of smartphones. The G7 simply takes smartphone ownership to the next level. Its slim design allows users to easily keep it in their pocket, and a headphone jack allows them to listen to music with their favourite headphones or earphones on the go!

Notable Features:

  • 6.1" QHD Display with FullVision
  • 16 MP/16 MP rear camera and 8 MP front camera
  • 3,000 mAh non-removable battery
  • 64GB internal memory; external memory added via microSD™ slot
  • 2.8GHz Octa-Core processor
